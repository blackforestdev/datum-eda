The right-click marking menu

Contiguous, semi-transparent wedges over a softly blurred board; the wheel auto-scales to its item count, and a submenu opens as its own radial while the parent layer fades and blurs behind it.

Right-clicked U1 · component wheel
hover to mark · click Align ▸ to descend · hub ◀ to return

Same gesture, two speeds

flick →Expert. Press and flick toward the verb — the wheel never draws, the command fires. Eyes stay on the board.
hold ~280msNovice. Press and wait — labeled wheel appears, drag to a slot. Every hold rehearses the expert flick.

Nested wheels & layers

A submenu is a new radial, not a list — Align ▸ opens its own wheel of Left / Right / Top / Bottom / Center-H / Center-V. The parent layer fades and blurs behind, glowing faintly through the semi-transparent wedges so you keep context.

Wedges auto-scale to item count (per object type, positions frozen for muscle memory). Past ~8 items or deep nesting, a More… wedge falls back to the linear overflow.
