Four configurations — the block scales with the sheet

Two positions (bottom band, right strip) × two orientations = four configs. The block is a constant proportion of the sheet — ~12% of the short edge — so a bigger sheet gets a proportionally bigger block. Same ratios, same Grauwert tone, every size.

Scaling — the block grows with the sheet

landscape band · true proportional scale
01Letter · Tabloid · 24×36 at one scaleband = 12% short edge, always
Shown at one common scale. The band is 12% of the short edge on every size, so it grows with the sheet — 32 mm on Letter, 41 on Tabloid, 90 on 24×36 — and the interior (type, hero, gutters) scales with it. On the big sheet the block is legible from across a plot room; on Letter it stays compact. The proportions — the look — never change.
